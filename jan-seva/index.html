WHO WE ARE

About Us

Jan Seva Foundation is a volunteer-based, non-profit organisation working in various social welfare fields including health, education, and empowerment. Founded with the mission to uplift underprivileged communities, our foundation is dedicated to creating a meaningful impact in society.

Our Vision

We aim to build a holistic support system for communities by promoting access to healthcare, education, and self-reliance. Our goal is to empower individuals to lead dignified and independent lives.

Our Programs

Education for All

We provide access to quality education for underprivileged children through free classes, school supplies, and awareness drives.

Health & Hygiene

Our regular health camps and hygiene awareness programs help ensure a healthier future for communities.

Women Empowerment

We support women with vocational training and support systems to help them achieve independence and dignity.

Environment Care

We run tree plantation drives, cleanliness missions, and campaigns to raise environmental awareness.
