Empowering Lives, One Step at a Time

Our Journey

Founded in 2020, Jan Seva Foundation is a non-profit committed to transforming the lives of underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, and women empowerment. Our goal is to ensure that every individual has access to a better life and equal opportunities.

With the help of volunteers and donors, we’ve reached thousands of families across India, providing support during times of crisis and creating long-lasting impact.
